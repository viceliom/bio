About

Vicélio Machaila is a computer scientist and Distinguished Teaching Professor of Computer Science at the Diogo Eugénio Guilande Technical University in Maputo, Mozambique. He also serves as Coordinator of the Faculty of Computer Sciences at the same institution.

He holds both a Bachelor of Science (BSc) and a Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science, earned in India, where he spent five years pursuing his academic training. His work focuses on advancing computer science education, integrating theory with practice, and fostering innovation in higher education.

Research Interests

  • Software Development and Programming (Python)
  • Information Systems and Organizational Technology
  • Corporate Engineering and Process Optimization
  • Digital Transformation in Education
  • Human-Computer Interaction and Systems Design

Teaching

  • Programming (Python, Visual Programming)
  • Information Systems
  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Systems

His teaching approach integrates theoretical instruction with hands-on practice, encouraging students to design, implement, and evaluate real-world systems.

Projects

  • Development of educational tools using Python (Tkinter)
  • Design of academic exercises and evaluation frameworks
  • Curriculum development for Computer Science programs
