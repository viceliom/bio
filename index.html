About

Vicélio Machaila is a computer scientist and Distinguished Teaching Lecturer of Computer Science at the Diogo Eugénio Guilande Technical University in Maputo, Mozambique. He also serves as Coordinator of the Faculty of Computer Sciences at the same institution.

He holds Bachelor of Science (BSc) and a Master of Science (MSc) degree in Computer Science, earned in India, where he spent five years pursuing his academic training. He also possess an Associate in dergree in Computer Systems from The Insitute of Computer Sciences and Administration, Maputo, Mozambique. His work focuses on advancing computer science education, integrating theory with practice, and fostering innovation in higher education.
